# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in WasmHatch a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Expected behavior

- Demonstrate empathy and kindness toward other people.
- Respect differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences.
- Give and gracefully accept constructive feedback.
- Accept responsibility, apologize to those affected by mistakes, and learn.
- Focus on what is best for the community and its users.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention.
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing another person's private information without explicit permission.
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately through a repository maintainer's
GitHub profile. Maintainers will investigate promptly and fairly, respect the
reporter's privacy, and may remove content or temporarily or permanently ban a
participant when necessary.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1, available at
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html>.
